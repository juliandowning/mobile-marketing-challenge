NourishBox is a UK subscription company delivering healthy snack boxes. Growth has slowed. Advertising costs are rising. Many customers cancel early.

Your task: build a profitable mobile-first strategy.

📋 Economic Constraints
NourishBox
Monthly fee: £25  ·  Gross margin: 60%  ·  Monthly profit contribution: £15
LTV = £15 × months retained  ·  Profit = LTV − Final CPA
Your decisions shift both CPA and retention. Make them count.
What's the aim of the game?
The Strategy Performance Index (SPI) is your score out of 120. It measures how well your decisions drive profitable, sustainable growth. It combines your financial outcome (profit per customer), retention quality (months kept), strategic alignment (do your choices reinforce each other?), risk calibration (does your risk level match your result?), your written reasoning, and deducts points for poor execution decisions.
